Hearty Minestrone Soup

Description

Minestrone soup is a traditional Italian soup containing beans, vegetables, and pasta in a tomato-based broth. Serve this with bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1 (32 ounce) container chicken broth
  • 1 (19 ounce) can cannelli beans
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups cubed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a heavy saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add onion, carrots, and celery; saute until beginning to soften, 3 to 4 minutes
  3. Add broth, beans, tomatoes, potatoes, cabbage, parsley, tomato paste, garlic, and salt
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until vegetables are tender (1 hour)
  6. Add pasta and simmer for 30 minutes more; adjust seasoning as needed
  7. Ladle into bowls and garnish with Parmesan cheese
