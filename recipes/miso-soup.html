Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a tasty, savory, and healthy Japanese soup dish. It is one of the most commonly-consumed meal items in Japan and makes an excellent snack, side dish, or full meal. You can use yellow, white, or red miso paste for this soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons daishi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch piecess

Steps

  1. Combine water and daishi granules in a medium saucepan
  2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste
  4. Stir in tofu
  5. Separate the layers of green onions and add them to the soup
  6. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving
